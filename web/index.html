Rev Sweeper Revolved

Windows download Source

A fixed-screen, all-range shooter built around precise aiming, an orbital weapon, dense enemy waves, and bullet-time pressure effects.

Keyboard and mouse

  • Move: WASD or arrow keys
  • Aim: mouse cursor
  • Fire: hold the left mouse button
  • Orbital Weapon: right mouse button
  • Confirm: left mouse button or Enter
  • Back/quit: Esc
  • Fullscreen: title-screen fullscreen icon

Gamepad

  • Move: left stick or D-pad
  • Aim direction: right stick
  • Fire: LB or LT
  • Orbital Weapon: RB or RT
  • Confirm: LB, LT, A/Cross, or Start
  • Back/quit: B/Circle or Back/Select

Orbital Weapon

Press once to deploy it around the ship, again to launch it, and once more to recall it. With a mouse it travels toward the cursor. With a gamepad, steer its direction with the right stick. It also returns automatically when its energy runs out.

Game modes

Arcade

Fight through increasingly intense enemy waves and boss battles in a finite run. Defeat the final boss to clear the game.

Endless

Survive an unending stream of enemies at the selected difficulty. The run continues until all lives are lost.

Scoring

Destroying enemies increases both your score and Tension. Higher Tension raises the value of subsequent kills, but gradually falls over time. Score milestones award extra lives.
